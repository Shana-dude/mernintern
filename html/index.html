Theri

Theri is a 2016 Tamil action drama starring Thalapathy Vijay and directed by Atlee. Vijay plays DCP Vijay Kumar, a dedicated and brave police officer who goes into hiding to protect his young daughter after a tragic event. The film beautifully blends action, emotion, and father–daughter bonding, showing Vijay in a powerful and caring role. Samantha and Amy Jackson play important supporting characters. Theri became a massive box-office success, praised for Vijay’s performance, memorable dialogues, stylish action scenes, and emotional storytelling, making it one of the most celebrated films in his career.

Naalaiya Theerpu

Naalaiya Theerpu is the debut film of Thalapathy Vijay, released in 1992. Directed by his father, S. A. Chandrasekhar, the movie marked Vijay’s entry into Tamil cinema at a young age. The film revolves around a young man seeking justice against powerful criminals who have wronged his family. Though the film did not become a major commercial success, it played a significant role in introducing Vijay to the audience and gave him the platform to grow into one of Tamil cinema’s biggest stars. Today, Naalaiya Theerpu is remembered as the starting point of Vijay’s successful career.

Pokkiri

Pokkiri is a 2007 Tamil action film starring Thalapathy Vijay and directed by Prabhu Deva. The film follows a fearless and mysterious gangster named Tamizhan, who works for a powerful crime syndicate. As the story unfolds, his true identity as an undercover police officer is revealed. Pokkiri is known for its stylish action scenes, sharp dialogues, and Vijay’s charismatic performance. The film became a massive commercial hit and is still remembered for its iconic fight sequences, comedy moments, and Vijay’s energetic screen presence. It remains one of the most celebrated mass-action movies in his career.
