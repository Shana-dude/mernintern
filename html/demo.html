About Vijay

Thalapathy Vijay is one of the most influential and admired actors in Tamil cinema.

Born on June 22, 1974, in Chennai, his full name is Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar. Growing up in a creative family with director S. A. Chandrasekhar and playback singer Shoba Chandrasekhar, Vijay developed a strong interest in films from a young age. He appeared as a child artist in several movies, gaining early confidence and camera experience. Vijay made his debut as a lead actor in 1992 with "Naalaiya Theerpu". Although the film did not succeed commercially, it marked the beginning of his inspiring journey. In his early career, Vijay faced challenges, criticism, and several setbacks. But his determination, hard work, and never-give-up attitude pushed him forward. His breakthrough came with the 1996 hit "Poove Unakkaga", which made him a favorite among family audiences. Vijay continued to grow with films like "Kadhalukku Mariyadhai", "Kushi", and "Thulladha Manamum Thullum". These movies showcased his natural acting, charm, and romantic hero image. His career reached new heights with the blockbuster "Ghilli" (2004), which established him as a powerful mass hero. Despite ups and downs in the mid-2000s, Vijay delivered strong performances and stayed committed to his craft. The 2007 movie "Pokkiri" became a massive commercial success and strengthened his mass appeal. His style, action, and screen presence were widely praised. A major turning point came in 2012 with "Thuppakki", directed by A.R. Murugadoss. The film became a milestone and expanded his fanbase across India and beyond. He later delivered several hits such as "Kaththi", "Theri", "Mersal", "Sarkar", "Master", and "Leo". These films showcased Vijay’s versatility and his ability to mix entertainment with strong social messages. Vijay is admired not only as an actor but also for his humble personality and humanitarian work. Through his *Vijay Makkal Iyakkam*, he supports education and welfare programs for underprivileged children. He often helps people quietly without seeking attention.

In 2024, Vijay surprised everyone by stepping into politics. His goal was to serve society directly and bring meaningful change. His political entry showed his commitment to public welfare and his willingness to take responsibility beyond cinema.

Today, Thalapathy Vijay stands as one of India’s biggest stars. His journey from a struggling debut actor to a global icon is a story of passion, dedication, and success. He continues to inspire millions of fans with his hard work, kindness, and leadership.

Movies

Thalapathy Vijay has acted in a wide variety of movies across romance, action, drama, and mass entertainment. His early films such as “Naalaiya Theerpu”, “Sendhoorapandi”, and “Rasigan” helped him enter the industry, while movies like “Poove Unakkaga” and “Kadhalukku Mariyadhai” established him as a romantic hero. Vijay’s emotional performance in “Thulladha Manamum Thullum” and “Love Today” increased his popularity among family audiences. He later transitioned into strong action roles with major hits like “Ghilli”, where he played a kabaddi champion, and “Thirupaachi”, which strengthened his mass image. The blockbuster “Pokkiri” showcased him as an undercover police officer and became one of the biggest hits in his career. With “Thuppakki”, Vijay delivered a stylish military character that received nationwide appreciation. Films like “Kaththi”, “Mersal”, and “Sarkar” carried strong social messages and earned both critical and commercial success.< Vijay impressed audiences with emotional depth in “Theri” and with powerful action in “Master”. His recent films, including “Bigil”, where he played a football coach, and “Leo”, a dark action thriller, showcased his versatility once again. Throughout his journey, Vijay has balanced romance, mass action, comedy, dance, emotional depth, and social themes, making him one of the most celebrated stars in Tamil cinema.
